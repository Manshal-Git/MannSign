How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to permanently delete your game account and associated data.

Quick steps

  1. 1

    Open the game

    Launch the game app on the device where your account is logged in.

  2. 2

    Go to the main dashboard screen

    If you are on a landing screen,navigate to app's main dashboard screen.

  3. 3

    Navigate to the Profile tab

    Tap on the profile tab(usually last in LTR layouts) to open your profile page.

  4. 4

    Tap the Settings (gear) icon

    Open the settings menu from within your profile.

  5. 5

    Select Account and Data

    Choose the Account and Data option to view account controls.

  6. 6

    Choose Delete Account

    Tap Delete Account to begin the deletion process.

  7. 7

    Provide confirmation

    Read the warning and confirm that you want to permanently delete your account. This action cannot be undone.

Important: Deleting your account will permanently remove your profile, progress, purchases, and any associated data.
