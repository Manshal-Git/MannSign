This Privacy Policy describes how Poke Puzzle: Tile Legends ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your data when you play our game. By using our game, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Gameplay Progress Data

We collect game progress, puzzle completion status, and puzzle statistics including move counts, elapsed time, and scores. This data is stored locally on your device to enable save and resume functionality.

b. Power-up Usage

We track which power-ups you've used and your inventory status (peek, undo, ghost, time-freeze) to provide a seamless gameplay experience and enable progression systems.

c. Device Information

We may collect basic device information (such as device model and OS version) for crash reporting and performance optimization purposes only.

d. Ad Personalization

Through the Google AdMob SDK, data such as user interests, demographics, and device information are collected for personalized ads. For more information, visit Google’s Ad Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Enable game save functionality and progress tracking
  • Track power-up inventory and usage
  • Optimize game performance and stability
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

All gameplay data is stored locally on your device using Android Room Database. We do not transmit your gameplay data to external servers unless you explicitly opt into cloud synchronization features (if available). Your data is encrypted at rest on your device using the operating system's built-in encryption.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services essential to the game’s functionality:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) – app performance and behaviorial analytics
  • Google AdMob – ad serving and analytics

5. User Rights and Controls

  • All your gameplay data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's cache through your device settings.
  • Opt-out of ad personalization via Google Ad Settings.
  • Request complete data deletion by following the device's app data deletion guide or by contacting manshalkhatri51@gmail.com.
  • All verified deletion requests result in permanent removal of your data from our systems.
