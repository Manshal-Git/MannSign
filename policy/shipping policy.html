Shipping Policy

Effective Date: 27-09-2023

Welcome to Mann Sign!

This Shipping Policy outlines important information regarding the shipment and delivery of your orders. Please read this policy carefully to understand our shipping process.

Order Processing

  • Manufacturing Timeline: Ordered products are manufactured within the timeline specified in your order details on the user app. We take pride in ensuring that your custom orders are crafted with care and precision.

Delivery

  • Delivery Time: Once your order is manufactured, the delivery time may vary depending on our courier partner company. Typically, delivery can take 5-6 days. Please note that delivery times may be subject to change based on factors such as location and courier availability.
  • Order Tracking: To keep you informed about the status of your order, we provide an order tracking link given by our courier partner. You can use this link to track your order and get real-time updates on its location and estimated delivery date.
  • Return Policy: Please be aware that once the product has been shipped, we do not offer a return facility. We encourage you to thoroughly review your order details before finalizing your purchase to ensure accuracy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Shipping Policy or the delivery of your order, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team. You can reach us from our customer support page.
