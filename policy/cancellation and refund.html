Cancellation and Refund Policy

Effective Date: 27-09-2023

Welcome to Mann Sign!

This Cancellation and Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding cancellations and refunds for orders placed through our online poster ordering app. Please read this policy carefully before placing an order. By placing an order, you agree to be bound by this policy.

1. Cancellations and Returns

Users have the right to cancel their order under the following conditions:

  • If the cancellation request is made within our app before making a payment for the confirmed order.
  • If the cancellation request is made from our customer support before order status is reached to processing or manufacturing state.

    • Note: There is no option for returning the delivered products

2. Refunds

Refunds will be issued under the following circumstances:

  • If the cancellation request meets the criteria mentioned above.
  • If there are any technical issues in the Payment process (e.g. your account has been deducted and payment status is not succeed).

The refund will be processed to the original payment method within 7 days of approving the refund request.

3. Refund Exclusions

Please note that refunds will not be provided under the following circumstances:

  • If the cancellation request is made after the specified time frame.
  • If the order has already been processed or shipped.
  • If the user is dissatisfied with the design or color choices made during the customization process.
  • If the user provides incorrect or incomplete delivery information resulting in non-delivery of the product.
