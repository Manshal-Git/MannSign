Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order for a premade poster?

To place an order for a premade poster, simply browse our collection, select the desired design, and proceed to the checkout page.

2. Can I customize a premade poster with my own text or images?

No, Currently premade posters cannot be customized with personalized text or images. They are available as-is and ready for purchase.

3. What are the available customization options for custom poster orders?

For custom poster orders, you can personalize the design by adding your own text, images, or choosing from available design elements, fonts, and colors.

4. How do I upload my own image for a custom poster order?

During the customization process, you will have the option to upload your own image. Simply click on the "Choose Image" button and follow the instructions to select and upload your desired image.

5. What are the accepted file formats for custom poster uploads?

We accept commonly used image file formats such as JPEG, PNG and PSD project files for custom poster uploads.

6. How long does it take to process and manufacture an order?

The processing and manufacturing time can vary depending on the complexity of the order and our current production schedule. We strive to complete orders within the estimated days that we mention at your order confirmation details you can check it within our app.

7. What are the shipping options and delivery timeframes?

We offer various shipping options based on your location and preferences. The delivery timeframes typically range from 2 to 3 days after your order get Ready(Manufactured) depending on our shipping partners.

8. How can I track the status of my order?

Once your order is shipped, we will provide you with a tracking url from our shipping partners. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your order through the shipping carrier's website.

9. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept online payments through major credit cards, debit cards via secure payment gateways like Stripe.

10. What is your cancellation and refund policy?

For information about our cancellation and refund policy, please refer to our Cancellation and Refund Policy.

11. What should I do if I receive a damaged or defective product?

In the event that you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact our customer support team immediately for assistance.
