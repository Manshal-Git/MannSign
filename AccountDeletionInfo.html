Account Deletion Process

Users who wish to request the deletion of their account can follow these steps:

  1. Open the app
  2. Open side menu and Go to the Profile screen
  3. Find the "Delete Account" option and tap on it.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the account deletion request.
    or
  • You can directly proceed to delete your account from here

Data Deletion and Retention

When a user requests account deletion, the following data will be deleted:

  • User profile information, such as name, email, and phone number.
  • App usage history and activity logs associated with the account.
  • Any personal data that the user provided during app usage.

Some data may be kept for a specific retention period to meet legal or regulatory requirements:

  • Transaction records for accounting purposes (retained for 7 years).
  • Aggregated, non-personal data used for analytics and app improvement.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your account and data, please contact our support team at mannsign09@gmail.com.
